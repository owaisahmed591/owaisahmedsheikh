What is an SEO audit
An SEO audit is a structured review of technical issues, content gaps, search intent, backlinks, internal links, and conversion paths that may be stopping a site from ranking.

My view: SEO should connect rankings to revenue. If a page ranks but does not attract the right customer, the strategy still needs work.
