FAQ

Medical Clinic SEO
Questions

Is it ethical to market a medical practice through SEO
Yes. SEO simply helps patients who are already searching for a doctor find you more easily. It is no different from a listing in a medical directory, just more effective. The content must be accurate, honest, and compliant with healthcare advertising guidelines - which is the approach I take.
How long does it take for a Karachi clinic to rank on Google Maps
Most Karachi clinics start seeing meaningful Google Maps ranking improvements within 6 to 12 weeks of proper GBP optimization and citation building. Full results depend on competition in your specialty and area.
I am a specialist doctor with a private practice. Can you help me specifically
Yes. Solo practitioner SEO is different from clinic SEO and in some ways easier - the Google Business Profile is for one person, the content is more focused, and the brand is more personal. I work with both solo practitioners and multi-doctor clinics.
Can SEO help me rank for a very competitive specialty like cardiology or oncology
Yes, but competitive specialties require a longer-term strategy. I start with area-specific and condition-specific searches where competition is lower, then build authority over time to target the broader specialty searches.
