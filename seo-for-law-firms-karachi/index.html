FAQ

Law Firm SEO
Questions

Which legal practice areas are most competitive for SEO in Karachi
Family law (divorce lawyers), criminal defense, and property law tend to be the most competitive because they have the highest search volume. Corporate law and intellectual property have lower search volume but also much lower competition - often easier to rank for.
How long does it take for a Karachi law firm to rank on page one
For less competitive practice areas, a Karachi law firm can appear on page one in 3 to 4 months. More competitive searches like "divorce lawyer Karachi" may take 6 to 9 months to reach page one consistently.
Should each lawyer at my firm have their own SEO profile page
Yes, if they each have different practice areas. Individual attorney profile pages with their specialties, qualifications, and experience optimize well for searches that include the type of case. These pages also build personal brand authority.
Can you help a solo advocate as well as a large law firm
Yes. The approach differs - a solo advocate benefits most from a highly focused local and niche practice area strategy, while a larger firm may target a broader range of practice areas across Karachi.
