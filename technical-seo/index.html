Technical SEO Issues I Fix

Common Problems That
Stop Sites From Ranking

Slow Page Speed

Google uses page speed as a ranking signal. I compress images, remove unused scripts, enable caching, and optimize your code to make your site load faster.

Crawl Errors

If Googlebot hits errors while crawling your site, your pages will not get indexed. I find and fix all crawl errors in Google Search Console.

HTTPS and Security

Sites without a valid SSL certificate or with mixed content warnings are penalized. I ensure your site is fully secured and properly configured.

Mobile Usability

Google indexes mobile-first. I check and fix mobile usability issues including tap targets, font sizes, viewport configuration, and mobile layout problems.

Indexing Problems

Noindex tags, canonical issues, and robots.txt blocks can prevent your pages from appearing in Google at all. I audit and fix all indexing-related issues.

Schema Markup

Schema helps Google understand your content and can unlock rich results in search. I add the right schema types for your business: LocalBusiness, FAQ, Service, and more.

Broken Links

Internal and external broken links waste your crawl budget and signal poor site maintenance to Google. I find and fix all broken links.

Core Web Vitals

LCP, INP, and CLS are Google's page experience metrics. Poor scores hurt rankings. I optimize your site to pass Core Web Vitals on both desktop and mobile.

XML Sitemap

A properly structured XML sitemap ensures Google finds and indexes all your important pages. I set up, fix, or optimize your sitemap and submit it to Search Console.

What is technical SEO
Technical SEO improves how search engines crawl, render, understand, and index your website. It covers site speed, broken pages, redirects, canonical tags, internal linking, schema, and Core Web Vitals.
