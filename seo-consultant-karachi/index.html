What is the difference between an SEO consultant and an SEO agency
An SEO agency has a team, usually with account managers, junior SEOs, and specialists. Your work gets distributed between people you may never speak to. An SEO consultant is one experienced person you work with directly. For most small and medium Karachi businesses, a consultant provides better value, more accountability, and clearer communication.
How much does an SEO consultant in Karachi charge
This depends on what you need. A one-time audit is a fixed cost. An ongoing monthly retainer depends on the scope of work. I do not publish standard prices because every business is different. Contact me for a free consultation and I will give you a clear quote based on your specific situation.
How quickly can I expect results from an SEO consultation
If you do a one-time consultation and implement the recommendations yourself, results depend on how quickly you implement and the competitiveness of your keywords. If I handle the ongoing work, most Karachi businesses start seeing ranking improvements in 3 to 4 months.
Do you offer SEO consulting for businesses outside Karachi
