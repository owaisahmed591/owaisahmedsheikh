Do I need a separate location page for my DHA Karachi business
If you serve customers in DHA specifically, a dedicated DHA location page significantly helps Google understand your geographic targeting. It captures searches like "best [service] in DHA" or "DHA Karachi [service] near me". I build and optimize these pages as part of local SEO.
How do I rank on Google Maps for DHA Karachi searches
Google Maps rankings in DHA Karachi depend on three main factors: your Google Business Profile relevance, your proximity to the searcher, and your overall authority (reviews, citations, website quality). I work on all three as part of local SEO for DHA businesses.
How long does it take to rank in DHA on Google Maps
Most DHA businesses start to see Google Maps ranking improvements within 2 to 3 months of proper local SEO work. Regular search result rankings (organic) typically take 3 to 6 months depending on competition in DHA.
