FAQ

Construction SEO
Questions

How long does construction company SEO take to generate leads
Most construction companies see their first organic leads within 3 to 5 months. The timeline depends on the competitiveness of your service types and your website's current technical state.
We work on large commercial projects, not residential. Can SEO still work for us
Yes, absolutely. Commercial construction SEO targets a smaller, more specific audience - developers, real estate companies, and institutional clients. The search volume is lower but the competition is also lower, and each lead is far more valuable.
Can you help a subcontractor, not just a general contractor
Yes. Subcontractors can rank for their specific trade - "plumbing contractor Karachi", "electrical installation company Karachi", "tiling contractor DHA". There is real search demand for trade-specific contractors in Karachi.
Our website is old and basic. Do we need a new website first
Not necessarily. I can often improve the SEO significantly on an existing website. However, if your website is technically broken or on a very old platform, a simple rebuild may be recommended as part of the strategy.
