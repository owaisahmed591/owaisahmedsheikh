Link Building Methods

How I Build Links
That Google Respects

Guest Posting

I write quality articles for relevant websites in your industry. These articles include a contextual link back to your site. Only real, editorially placed links, no link farms.

Backlink Audit

Before building new links, I audit your existing backlink profile. Toxic or spammy links from previous campaigns can hurt your rankings. I identify and disavow them.

Broken Link Building

I find broken links on relevant websites and offer your content as a replacement. This is a natural, effective way to earn links that benefits both parties.

Digital PR and Mentions

Getting your business mentioned in news articles, industry blogs, or local Karachi media builds both links and brand authority at the same time.

Resource and Directory Links

Legitimate industry directories and resource pages provide solid foundational links. I identify and submit to the ones that are actually trusted by Google.

No Black Hat Links

I do not sell link packages, use PBNs, or buy links from sketchy networks. These methods can cause a manual Google penalty that is very difficult to recover from.

Important

The Truth About
Link Building

A lot of people selling SEO in Pakistan offer link packages. 100 links for a cheap price, delivered in a week. Those links come from low-quality sites, link farms, or networks that Google has already identified. At best they do nothing. At worst they cause a penalty.

I also built the SEO Readiness Chrome extension, a tool used by link builders worldwide to check whether a site is actually being crawled by Google before placing a link there. A link on a site that Google is not actively crawling is worthless.

Real link building takes time. A genuine guest post on a relevant site with real traffic is worth hundreds of cheap directory links. I focus on quality, not quantity.

What Google Wants
Links should be earned because your content is useful, your business is real, and other websites want to reference you. That is what I help you build.
What is off-page SEO
Off-page SEO builds trust outside your website through quality backlinks, brand mentions, citations, and digital PR signals that help Google evaluate authority.

My view: SEO should connect rankings to revenue. If a page ranks but does not attract the right customer, the strategy still needs work.
