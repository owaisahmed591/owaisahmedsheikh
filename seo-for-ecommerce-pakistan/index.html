E-commerce SEO in Karachi

E-commerce SEO in
Pakistan: The Full Picture

Pakistan's e-commerce market is growing rapidly. More consumers are buying online every year, and Google is their primary discovery channel for new products and brands. Most Pakistan online stores, however, invest almost entirely in paid social media and influencer marketing, leaving the organic search channel largely untapped.

E-commerce SEO in Pakistan starts with the technical foundation. Many Pakistan online stores - particularly those on WooCommerce or custom solutions - have serious technical issues that prevent Google from crawling and indexing their products properly. These include duplicate content from URL parameters, missing canonical tags, crawl traps, and slow page speeds. Fixing these issues alone can cause significant ranking improvements.

After the technical foundation, the focus shifts to product and category page optimization. Category pages are the highest leverage pages for e-commerce SEO. A well-optimized category page targeting a high-volume commercial keyword like "men's kurta Pakistan" or "women's bags Karachi" can drive significant recurring organic traffic with strong purchase intent.

For established stores, link building accelerates results significantly. I focus on getting links from relevant Pakistan websites, bloggers, and industry publications that add real authority to your store's domain.

FAQ

E-commerce SEO
Pakistan FAQ

How long does e-commerce SEO take to show results in Pakistan
Technical fixes start showing improvements within 4 to 8 weeks. Product and category page rankings for competitive terms typically take 4 to 6 months. New stores generally take longer than established stores with existing authority.
Which e-commerce platforms work best for SEO in Pakistan
WooCommerce and Shopify both have solid SEO foundations. WooCommerce gives more control over technical elements. Regardless of platform, what matters more is how well the SEO is implemented, not which platform you use.
My store has 500 products. Can you optimize all of them
For large catalogs, we prioritize. I identify the product pages with the most ranking potential based on search volume, competition, and revenue value, and optimize those first. We then work through the catalog systematically.
Do you also do SEO for Pakistan marketplace stores like Daraz
Marketplace SEO (Daraz SEO) uses different principles than Google SEO. I focus on your own domain and website. However, I can advise on how to drive traffic from Google to your Daraz store listings as part of a broader strategy.
