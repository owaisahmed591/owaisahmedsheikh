Internal links help users and Google move through your website.

For service businesses, internal linking can decide which pages become strong enough to rank.

If your homepage, blogs, and service pages do not connect properly, Google may not understand which pages matter most.

## Start with your money pages

Money pages are the pages that can generate leads.

These usually include service pages, local landing pages, audit pages, consultation pages, and industry pages.

### Examples

- Local SEO Karachi
- Technical SEO
- SEO Content Writing
- SEO Audit
- SEO for Clinics Karachi

## Use descriptive anchor text

Anchor text tells users what they will get after clicking.

Avoid vague text like "click here" when a clearer phrase makes sense.

Better examples include SEO content writing service, technical SEO audit, local SEO for Karachi businesses, and Google Business Profile checklist.

## Link from blogs to services

Blog posts should not sit alone.

If a blog explains a problem, link to the service that solves it. If a blog answers a question, link to the deeper guide or case study.

#### Example

A post about old blog content should link to SEO content writing, content audits, and related articles about topical authority.

## Build topic clusters

A topic cluster connects a main service page with supporting blog posts.

For local SEO, the cluster might include Google Business Profile, reviews, citations, local landing pages, service area pages, and Search Console tracking.

## Do not overdo it

Too many links can make a page messy.

Use links where they help the reader. A good internal link feels useful, not forced.

## Final advice

Internal linking is one of the simplest SEO improvements.

You already own the links. Use them to guide people from helpful information to the pages that can turn interest into leads.

## Common mistakes to avoid

The biggest mistake with internal linking is treating it like a checklist with no business context.

You can pass a tool check and still leave the customer confused. You can also publish more content and still miss the search intent. Good SEO work connects the technical detail to a real user problem.

### Mistake to watch

Avoid adding links only because a keyword appears. It usually creates more noise than progress.

A better approach is to make one useful improvement, measure it, and then move to the next page or section.

## How this connects to revenue

internal linking should help the business get more qualified visitors, clearer leads, and better trust. If the work does not support one of those outcomes, it is probably not the next priority.

For a local business in Pakistan, that might mean more calls from Google Maps. For an ecommerce store, it might mean more category page clicks. For a service company, it might mean better consultation requests from people who already understand the offer.

## What to do next

Start small. pick your five most important service pages and make sure each one receives links from relevant blogs, the homepage, and related service pages.

After that, check Search Console and user behavior. If impressions improve but clicks stay weak, improve the title and meta description. If clicks improve but leads do not, improve the page copy, proof, and CTA.

SEO gets easier when every update has a clear reason.
