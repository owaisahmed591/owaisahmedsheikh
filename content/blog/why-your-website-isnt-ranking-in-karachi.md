Most Karachi business owners come to SEO with the same question.

Why is my website live, but still not showing on Google?

The answer is rarely one single thing. Usually the site has a mix of local SEO gaps, weak content, technical issues, and missing trust signals. The good news is that these problems can be fixed in a clear order.

This guide explains the issues I see most often when auditing websites for businesses in Karachi and across Pakistan.

## 1. Your Google Business Profile is incomplete

If you serve local customers, your Google Business Profile matters as much as your website.

Many businesses have a profile, but it is half finished. The category is too broad. Services are missing. Photos are old. Reviews are not being answered. The website link goes to the homepage even when a better service page exists.

### What to fix first

- Choose the most accurate primary category
- Add service areas and business hours
- Write a clear business description
- Upload real photos of your work, office, team, or products
- Add every important service with a short description
- Reply to reviews in a natural way

#### Quick check

Search your main service plus your area, like "dentist in Gulshan Karachi" or "SEO consultant Karachi". If competitors have stronger profiles, your Google Maps SEO needs work.

## 2. Your website has technical SEO problems

A site can look fine to a person and still be hard for Google to crawl.

Common technical SEO issues include slow mobile pages, broken internal links, missing sitemap files, duplicate pages, noindex tags, weak canonical tags, and heavy images.

### What a healthy site should do

Google should be able to crawl your pages, understand the main topic, index the right version, and move through your internal links without confusion.

If your important pages are not indexed, rankings will not happen.

## 3. Your keywords are too broad

A small business usually should not begin with the biggest keyword in the market.

Trying to rank for "restaurant Karachi", "lawyer Karachi", or "real estate Pakistan" is hard because those searches are broad and competitive.

Better keyword targeting is more specific.

- Family restaurant in DHA Karachi
- Skin clinic in Gulshan Karachi
- Real estate agent in Bahria Town Karachi
- SEO consultant for small businesses in Karachi

## 4. Your service pages are thin

A weak service page usually says what the business does, but not enough to help the buyer decide.

Strong SEO content explains the service in simple language. It answers the questions a real customer asks before calling.

### A better service page includes

- The exact service offered
- Who it is for
- What problem it solves
- What the process looks like
- Local areas served
- Proof, examples, or reviews
- Clear next step

This is where SEO content writing matters. The goal is not to repeat keywords. The goal is to make the page useful, clear, and complete.

## 5. Your site has no authority signals

Backlinks, mentions, reviews, case studies, and useful resources all help build trust.

A small website with no links and no proof will struggle against competitors that have been visible for years.

### Safer authority sources

- Local citations
- Partner mentions
- Guest posts on relevant sites
- Case studies
- Useful guides that people can reference
- Reviews from real customers

Avoid bulk link packages. They usually create more risk than value.

## What I would fix first

Start with the highest-impact basics.

Fix the Google Business Profile. Audit the technical SEO. Improve the main service pages. Add internal links. Then build authority slowly.

If you are not sure where to begin, get a proper SEO audit first. Random SEO work is expensive when the main problem is still hidden.
