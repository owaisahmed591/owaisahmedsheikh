FAQ

Local SEO Questions
Answered

How long does local SEO take in Karachi
Most businesses start seeing Google Maps ranking improvements within 2 to 3 months. More competitive areas like DHA or Clifton may take 4 to 5 months. Full results usually show up in 4 to 6 months with consistent work.
My business is not on Google Maps. Can you add it
Yes. I can set up your Google Business Profile from scratch, get it verified, and then fully optimize it so it has the best chance of ranking in the local 3-pack.
Do I need a physical address in Karachi
For Google Maps rankings, yes. Google requires a real address to verify your business. If you work from home and do not want to share your address publicly, there are options to set a service area instead.
Can you help with specific Karachi areas like DHA or Gulshan
Yes. I can build area-specific landing pages and optimize your content for searches like "dentist in DHA Karachi" or "restaurant in Gulshan-e-Iqbal" depending on where your customers are.
What is the Google local 3-pack
The local 3-pack is the box that appears on Google showing three local businesses on a map when someone searches for a local service. It appears before regular organic results and gets the highest click rates. Getting into the 3-pack is the primary goal of local SEO.
