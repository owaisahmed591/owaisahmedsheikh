General SEO

The Basics

What is SEO and why does it matter for my business
SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It is the process of making your website more visible in Google search results. When someone in Karachi searches for a product or service you offer, SEO is what determines whether your site appears on page one or page ten. Most people never scroll past the first page. If you are not there, you are missing potential customers every single day.
How long does SEO take to show results
This depends on how competitive your industry is and the current state of your website. In most cases, you will start seeing ranking improvements in 3 to 4 months. Meaningful traffic growth usually appears around month 5 or 6. For very competitive keywords, it can take 8 to 12 months. SEO is a long-term investment. The results compound over time, unlike paid ads which stop the moment you stop paying.
Can you guarantee page one rankings
No. Anyone who makes this guarantee is not being honest with you. No SEO professional has control over Google's algorithm. What I can tell you is that I follow the practices that consistently improve rankings, I track everything transparently, and if something is not working I adjust the approach. I have a strong track record, but I will never promise a specific position.
Is SEO better than Google Ads (PPC)
They are different and both have their place. Google Ads give you immediate visibility but stop the moment you stop paying. SEO takes time but the traffic you build is free and continues growing. Most businesses benefit from using both together. Start with SEO for long-term growth and use ads to get immediate leads while the SEO work takes hold.
What is the difference between white-hat and black-hat SEO
White-hat SEO means following Google's guidelines: creating useful content, earning real backlinks, improving site speed and user experience. Black-hat SEO means taking shortcuts: buying low-quality links, keyword stuffing, and manipulating rankings in ways Google has specifically warned against. White-hat results take longer but last. Black-hat results are fast but often end in a Google penalty that can be very difficult to recover from.

Local SEO

For Karachi Businesses

What is local SEO and how is it different from regular SEO
Local SEO focuses specifically on ranking in searches that include a location, like "dentist in Karachi" or "best restaurant DHA Karachi". It also covers Google Maps rankings. Regular SEO targets broader keywords without a geographic focus. If you have a physical business or serve a specific city, local SEO is usually your highest priority because the customers searching locally are ready to take action.
How do I get my business into Google's local 3-pack
The local 3-pack is the box showing three businesses on a map in local search results. To rank there, you need a properly optimized Google Business Profile, consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) information across the web, strong reviews, and your website needs to be relevant to the local search intent. It typically takes 2 to 4 months of consistent local SEO work to enter the 3-pack for competitive Karachi searches.
How important are Google reviews for local rankings
Very important. Reviews are one of the key signals Google uses to rank local businesses. More reviews and higher average ratings generally lead to better local rankings. Responding to reviews also signals to Google that your business is active and engaged. I help clients set up a simple system to consistently get new genuine reviews from happy customers.
