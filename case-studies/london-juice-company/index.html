The Situation

London Juice Company had product breadth and commercial relevance, but technical SEO and crawl efficiency were holding the site back. The project focused on making the site easier to index, easier to understand, and more commercially useful.

The work covered sitemap handling, technical cleanup, internal linking, and authority-building support so the site could compete better for product and manufacturer-intent searches.

What needed to change
  • Important pages needed stronger technical compliance and crawl support
  • Commercial pages were not fully optimized around buyer intent
  • The site needed a better authority and internal-linking foundation
  • Search performance needed clearer reporting and prioritization
What I worked on
  • Reviewed sitemap and page-indexing performance in Search Console
  • Audited crawl health, broken links, URL structure, and page-level technical issues
  • Improved commercial positioning across priority pages
  • Supported authority-building through directories, profile consistency, and outreach prep
