Luxury Furniture - SEO + Paid Search

The Situation

Handcrafted Thrones needed growth, not just traffic. The website had weak organic visibility, ad spend was too broad, and the business was not consistently showing up for the commercial searches most likely to lead to sales.

The work combined SEO and paid refinement. That meant narrowing the keyword strategy, building out pages for high-intent searches, and tightening campaigns so the business attracted buyers instead of casual browsers.

What needed to change
  • Low organic visibility for commercial throne-related searches
  • Ad spend was broad and produced weak-quality traffic
  • Core product pages were not aligned to revenue-driving keywords
  • The business needed measurable revenue growth inside six months
What I worked on
  • Targeted high-intent keywords such as custom throne for sale and luxury event thrones
  • Improved landing pages and commercial copy for purchase-ready traffic
  • Refined Meta and Google Ads targeting and creative testing
  • Secured relevant backlinks from design, event, and industry websites
