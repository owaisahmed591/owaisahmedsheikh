Mental Health Speaker - Lead Generation

The Situation

Tonier Cain International already had brand recognition, but the site mostly attracted people who already knew Tonier's work. The bigger opportunity was to reach schools, universities, corporate planners, and organizations actively searching for a keynote speaker on mental health, trauma, and wellness.

The SEO strategy focused on demand capture. Instead of relying on branded traffic, the campaign built landing pages and blog content around the real language used by event planners and institutions looking for a speaker.

What needed to change
  • Strong branded visibility but weak non-branded discovery
  • No dedicated pages for high-intent keynote speaker terms
  • Limited content targeting decision-makers in education and corporate sectors
  • Missed local opportunity for Los Angeles-based speaker searches
What I worked on
  • Built dedicated landing pages for mental health keynote speaker intent and Los Angeles searches
  • Published blog content around schools, universities, workplace productivity, and trauma-informed care
  • Improved on-page SEO and internal links so content supported conversion pages
  • Used social amplification to drive additional traffic back to speaking pages
