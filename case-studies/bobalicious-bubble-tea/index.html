Wholesale Bubble Tea - Technical + Content SEO

The Situation

Bobalicious had a strong product story, but the site needed better technical structure, stronger title and meta optimization, and tighter alignment between commercial pages and search intent.

The project focused on technical compliance, organic visibility, and better support for the pages most likely to drive wholesale inquiries.

What needed to change
  • Commercial pages needed stronger search intent alignment
  • Technical warnings and crawl issues reduced efficiency
  • The site needed clearer CTR opportunities on pages with high impressions
  • The brand depended heavily on mobile and international search audiences
What I worked on
  • Audited technical issues and site-health warnings
  • Reviewed titles, metas, FAQs, and structured-data opportunities on key pages
  • Focused on commercial intent for wholesale and product pages
  • Used Search Console and SEO reporting to identify high-impression pages with CTR upside

What Changed
