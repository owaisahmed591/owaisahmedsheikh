Beverage Brand - Product Discovery

The Situation

Mr Basil already had a strong product identity, but the site structure and commercial messaging were not fully aligned with how buyers, distributors, and searchers were looking for the brand online.

The strategy focused on making the product range easier to understand for Google and for buyers. That meant improving page intent, strengthening product and distributor paths, and using Search Console data to understand where branded demand was already building.

What needed to change
  • Homepage messaging did not clearly capture manufacturer and distributor intent
  • Important supporting pages like distributor and certifications were missing or underdeveloped
  • Product pages lacked procurement and buyer-focused information
  • The site needed better internal paths from informational content into commercial pages
What I worked on
  • Reframed the homepage and supporting pages around manufacturer, distributor, wholesale, and export intent
  • Planned and prioritized missing distributor and certification content
  • Reviewed product page structure and procurement detail gaps
  • Used Search Console data to track query growth and priority page performance

What Changed

  • Google Search Console reported 967 clicks and 127K impressions in the approved snapshot
