Done Properly

Keyword Research

Finding the right keywords is the foundation. I research what your customers are actually searching for in Karachi and Pakistan, including search volume, competition level, and buying intent.

Title Tags and Meta Descriptions

Your title tag is the most important on-page element. I write keyword-optimized titles and compelling meta descriptions that improve both rankings and click-through rates.

Heading Structure

Proper H1, H2, and H3 structure helps Google understand what your page is about. I fix heading hierarchy issues and include keywords where they make sense naturally.

Content Optimization

I review existing content for keyword gaps, thin pages, and quality issues. I tell you exactly what to add, remove, or rewrite to make each page more competitive.

Internal Linking

A smart internal linking structure passes authority between your pages and helps Google discover all your content. I build a linking plan that supports your most important pages.

Image Optimization

Images with missing or poor alt text miss opportunities to rank in Google image search. I also check for uncompressed images that are slowing down your pages.

Process

How I Approach
On-Page Optimization

01
Keyword Mapping

I map one primary keyword to each page on your site. No two pages compete for the same keyword. This removes cannibalization and focuses Google's attention properly.

02
Competitor Content Review

I check the top three pages ranking for your target keywords. What topics do they cover What questions do they answer I use this to make your content more complete.

03
Page-by-Page Optimization

I go through each important page: title, meta, headings, content, images, and links. I provide a detailed document with all changes needed.

04
Implementation and Tracking

I implement the changes or guide your team through implementation. Then I track ranking changes over the following months to measure impact.

What is on-page SEO
On-page SEO is the process of making each page clearly match a searcher's intent through better titles, headings, content structure, internal links, and keyword targeting.

My view: SEO should connect rankings to revenue. If a page ranks but does not attract the right customer, the strategy still needs work.
